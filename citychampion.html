ABOUT US

City champion is an initiative for enhancement of student skill set from very root levels .This is a contest which is organized by student community ofAditya Institute of Technology and Management , Tekkali. The strategy of iniating this contest is to declare one school as city champion among many schools around the city of Srikakulam on the basis of student performance in all the aspects of competition organized by the AITAM. This contest focuses on selecting of effective and interested students from different schools to make them learn the advanced technology at very basic stages in the forms of Summer Carnivals at the workspace of Aditya Institute of Technology and Management. This contest will make students to know the current technologies and the advancements in Technology which are going to enhance future of our society. Students who are going to build this nation need to be very much advanced in all the aspects so that they can learn the technology in schooling to develop products beneficial for their school in an innovative and creative way.
